Sales Pitch
What to say — and how to say it
Handle every prospect scenario with confidence
The 30-Second Pitch
"Ask Empower V2 is your company's conversational intelligence engine. Instead of searching through recordings, you simply ask — about a client, a rep, a deal, a trend, an objection. It answers from the full history of your team's calls and meetings. Your sales reps close faster, your managers coach smarter, and your entire team stops losing context between conversations."
Handle the Hard Questions
❓ "Can it update my CRM automatically?"
Yes. Call summaries and action items sync to your CRM today for both calls and video meetings. CRM auto-fill (field enrichment) for both calls and video meetings ships in April 2026. Full bi-directional sync is on our roadmap.
❓ "We already use Fireflies / Fathom."
Those are meeting recorders. Empower is conversational intelligence. Ask it about your team's top objections across 3 months, or which reps need MEDDIC coaching. Your current tool can't do that.
❓ "How is this different from Modjo?"
Modjo is a good tool — but it integrates with your phone system. Empower is your phone system. 100% call capture, natively. Lower cost, no seat minimums, covering sales, support, and recruitment.
❓ "We don't use Ringover."
Empower works standalone. Native integrations with Aircall and RingCentral. Any other call system can push via our public upload API. No need to switch phone providers.

⚠️ What NOT to Promise
Do not position Empower as a revenue intelligence or forecasting tool yet. Do not promise that Ask Empower can accurately predict deal probability, pipeline health, or forecast accuracy — this requires CRM data not yet connected.

The right framing: "Empower is the conversational intelligence foundation that revenue intelligence is built on. We're building it phase by phase — and Phase 4 (full CRM sync) lands in April 2026."
